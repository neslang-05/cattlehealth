About the Data

This dashboard displays live data streamed directly from the health monitoring sensor attached to Cow #1. The values update in real-time as new readings are received from the device.

  • Pulse: The raw pulse reading from the sensor.
  • Internal Temp: The internal body temperature of the cow, measured in Celsius.
  • External Temp: The ambient temperature of the cow's immediate surroundings.
  • Last Update: The time the last data point was received.
